ABOUT THE FOUNDER

Gini Group was formed in 1963 by late Mr. Vishwanath Harlalka, in Mumbai. It is primarily involved in the manufacture of Textile Fabrics. For the past 55 years the simple idea of combining quality with affordability has been safeguarded as the core of our business identity. Today we have grown into one of the leading manufacturers of Shirting Fabrics having a pan India distribution network and an ever-growing global presence. For the last five decades, Gini has been the market leader in shirting fabrics. Taking inspiration from this success and the growing consumer demand for better housing in India, Gini Constructions was formed.

COMPANY HISTORY

Gini Constructions is a part of the Gini Group of Companies located in Maharashtra. Gini Group was formed in 1963 by late Mr. Vishwanath Harlalka, in Mumbai and is primarily involved in the manufacture of Textile Fabrics. The business commenced with a humble idea of providing customers with a quality product at a reasonable price. For the past 55 years this simple idea has been safeguarded as the core of our business identity and today we have grown into one of the leading manufacturers of Shirting Fabrics having a pan India distribution network as well as a strong presence in the global markets.
